Parichay 2023

On September 23, 2023, the Department of Computer Applications hosted "Parichay," a vibrant freshers party welcoming BCA and MCA students. The event buzzed with excitement as new students were introduced to their academic journey. Parichay offered a platform for networking, fostering friendships, and initiating a sense of belonging. It featured interactive activities, performances, and inspiring speeches, creating a memorable experience for the incoming students.
