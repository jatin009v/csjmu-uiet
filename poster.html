Poster Exhibition

The Department of Computer Applications is hosting a captivating poster exhibition on the themes of nature, saving Earth, and global warming. The event showcases intricate handmade posters depicting the beauty of nature and urging viewers to save our planet. Through vibrant visuals and compelling messages, the exhibition aims to raise awareness about the urgent need to combat global warming and preserve the environment for future generations.
