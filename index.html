Department Of Computer Applications

About Department

To cater to the ever growing demands for software professionals in the emerging field of computer software, the Department of Computer Applications was started in the year 2001. The department was started with two courses namely Master of Computer Application (MCA) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) having 60 and 30 seats respectively.

The department has been continually growing since the beginning. Currently the department also runs a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) program along with the aforesaid programs. The current intake of MCA is 66 (60+6 EWS seats) and that of BCA is 330 (300+30 EWS seats). The syllabus of the program has been designed with the help of experienced IT professionals and academicians from the renowned institutes. It incorporates subjects from the field of Computer Science, Management and Communication. The department has 4 state of the art laboratories equipped with the latest software. The syllabus of the program is monitored and updated on a regular basis.
