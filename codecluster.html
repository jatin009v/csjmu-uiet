Code Cluster 2023

"CSJM University hosted a Code cluster contest with the presence of the Vice-Chancellor and Registrar, where students from various departments participated. The practice session took place on October 16, with finals on October 17. The students showcased their talents in different fields, and the event encouraged their skills and camaraderie. VC emphasized the importance of such competitions. The successful contest has inspired CSJM to continue promoting such opportunities for students."
