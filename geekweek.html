Geek वीक (Code Vidya)

Geek Week at CSJMU Campus, scheduled from 20th to 25th March, promises an exhilarating tech experience. The event features a diverse range of programs, including "Tech Whiz" for tech enthusiasts, "Fashionova" for fashion-tech fusion, and "Code Warz" for intense coding challenges. Other highlights include "Junkyard Junkies" for creative engineering, "Web Dev Set" for web development enthusiasts, and "Keyboard Warriors" for competitive typing. Participants can also engage in "Rebootal" extempore , "Biz Wiz" entrepreneurship challenges, and the thrilling "Hack n Hunt." Additionally, there are events like "Rubix Cubix" for puzzle enthusiasts, "Expert Connect" for knowledge sharing, and "Crosshair Combat" for gaming aficionados. GEEK WEEK offers a blend of technology, creativity, and innovation. Participants can showcase their skills, connect with experts, and engage in spirited competitions, making it a must-attend event for tech enthusiasts and aspiring professionals.
