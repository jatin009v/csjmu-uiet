DOROTICS CONCLAVE

On March 31, 2023, the 2nd National Drone Ranking was announced, showcasing cutting-edge drone technology achievements. The event highlighted advancements in aerial robotics, promoting innovation and skill development. Following this, on April 1, 2023, the Dorotics Conclave took place, gathering experts and enthusiasts to explore the future of drone technology, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange among participants.
