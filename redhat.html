Red Hat

On April 26th, 2023, the Department of Computer Applications organized an interactive talk on Red Hat featuring Mr. Sanjay Srivastava, Head of Public Sector, Asia Pacific, as the guest speaker. The event provided valuable insights into Red Hat technologies and their applications. Students and faculty engaged in an enriching discussion, gaining knowledge from Mr. Srivastava's expertise in the field of open-source solutions.
