Code Cluster 2023

"CSJM University hosted a Code cluster contest with the presence of the Vice-Chancellor and Registrar, where students from various departments participated. The practice session took place on October 16, with finals on October 17. The students showcased their talents in different fields, and the event encouraged their skills and camaraderie. VC emphasized the importance of such competitions. The successful contest has inspired CSJM to continue promoting such opportunities for students."

Parichay 2023

"In 2023, CSJM University hosted an event to introduce BCA and MCA students to the academic year. The event witnessed the presence of the Vice-Chancellor, along with the Head of the Department (HOD) and other faculty members. Notably, the Vice-Chancellor's wife also graced the occasion, making it a memorable gathering for the students."

Chandryan 3

Srijan Pal Singh, from the Abdul Kalam Center, Engages CSJM University Students in Chandrayaan-3 Discussion Kanpur, Date: Srijan Pal Singh, a prominent figure associated with the Abdul Kalam Center, recently visited Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University (CSJM) to interact with the students. The highlight of his visit was an engaging discussion on the Chandrayaan-3 mission. Srijan Pal Singh shared insights and updates on the highly anticipated lunar mission, Chandrayaan-3, with the enthusiastic CSJM University students. He emphasized the significance of India's space exploration endeavors and encouraged the students to take a keen interest in the nation's scientific achievements. The interactive session with Srijan Pal Singh left the students inspired and enlightened, as they gained a deeper understanding of India's space exploration initiatives. It was a significant step in fostering scientific curiosity among the future leaders.
